    • Animated Poster

    I collaborated with comedian and designer Sam Nicoresti or the artist formerly known as Soggy Milk Design. He designed this incredible poster on Photoshop and Illustrator and sent me the the PSD file. I imagined the eye rolling into view and squeezing out a lightning bolt. This is my favourite element in the poster. The joystick is cool too, but really we're here for the eye aren't we? I had so much fun with this project because I could concentrate on the motion. I'm learning design, photoshop and illustrator but there's nothing like working with someone who knows what they're doing.
